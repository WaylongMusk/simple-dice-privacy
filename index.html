Privacy Policy for Simple Dice

Last updated: January 2024

Overview

Simple Dice is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

Information Collection

Simple Dice does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. We do not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Track your location
  • Use analytics services
  • Store any user data
  • Require any account creation
  • Use third-party services

Device Permissions

The app only requires access to your device's motion and acceleration sensors for the shake-to-roll feature. This data is only used locally and is not stored or transmitted.

Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages and does not collect any information from children or adults.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
